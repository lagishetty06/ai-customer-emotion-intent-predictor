## 💡 Recording Tips:
* **Preparation**: Open the app at `http://localhost:8501` and have your browser screen zoomed to about `110%` or `120%` so the text is large and clear.
* **Typing Speed**: To keep the video exactly under 60 seconds, copy and paste the example sentences rather than typing them out key-by-key.
* **Resolution**: Record in full HD (1080p) to keep the text sharp and readable on mobile devices.
